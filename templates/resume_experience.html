Why Work Experience Matters

Your work history demonstrates your skills in action and shows career progression. It's the most important section on your resume.

Tips for Great Work Experience

  • Focus on achievements rather than just duties
  • Use numbers and metrics to quantify your impact
  • Start bullet points with strong action verbs
  • Tailor your descriptions to highlight skills relevant to the job
  • List experiences in reverse chronological order (newest first)
  • Include 3-5 bullet points per position

Example

Senior Software Engineer

TechCorp Inc.

Jan 2020 - Present

  • Led the development of a payment processing system that reduced transaction errors by 35%
  • Mentored 5 junior developers, improving team productivity by 20%
  • Optimized database queries, decreasing load times by 40%
  • Implemented automated testing, reducing bugs in production by 25%
